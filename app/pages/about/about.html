About People On this page, you're able to find an alfabetic list of all the users who are stored on the server and have both a mail-adress and a phonenumber.
Besides viewing the users you can also import/save the users to your phones contactlist, direct call or text the users and share the contact information in a textformat. Contact This page provides you a list with all the contacts that are stored on your phone.
Just like the People-page it doesn't stop at viewing the contacts, you can perform all the actions that you have on the peoples page except for import/save since you already have the contacts stored on your phone. Profile Here we will check if you have already uploaded a profile picture on sharepoint. If so you will see that photo, if not you'll see a default picture and a notification to upload a picture as soon as possible.
You can also perform a logout from this page.
